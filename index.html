Mikaela Roberts

Mikaela is a Canadian filmmaker, photographer, and graphic designer based out of Ottawa, Ontario. She graduated from Concordia University’s Mel Hoppenheim School of Cinema with a BFA in Film Production, and is currently studying Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College. In her work, Mikaela seeks to highlight unique identities, and tell visual stories that matter.

Mikaela works as a director of her own projects and collaborator as a videographer and editor. Her most recent film, Pappy’s Garden (2021), has screened at international film festivals such as Hot Docs 2022, and is currently being distributed by the Canadian Filmmakers Distribution Centre (CFMDC) for a 2022-23 festival run.

Mikaela grew up in Basel, Switzerland, before returning to Canada to pursue her university studies. Mikaela is an avid traveler and loves hiking and discovering new landscapes, histories, and cultures.
